Information about 1mg Immune Support Capsule

1mg Immune Support Capsule is scientifically formulated with seven well-known immunity boosting herbal ingredients such as tulsi, giloy, munakka, turmeric, vasaka, shunthi, and kali mirch. It boosts immunity as well as activates the immune system response. Moreover, it may reduce the chances of bacterial or viral infections.

Key Ingredients:
  • Tulsi (Ocimum sanctum) leaves ext. - 5% Tannins 100mg
  • Giloy (Tinospora cordifolia) stem ext. – 0.5% Bitters 100mg
  • Munakka (Vitis vinifera) ext. 75 mg
  • Turmeric (Curcuma longa) rhizome ext. - 20% Curcuminoids 50mg
  • Vasaka (Adhatoda vasaka) leaves ext. – 0.5% Volatile oil 50mg
  • Shunthi (Zingiber officinale) rhizome ext. – 2% Gingerols 25mg
  • Kali Mirch (Piper nigrum) fruit ext. – 5% Piperine 25mg
Key Benefits:
  • Helps to boost the immunity level of the body
  • Helps to activate the immune system response
  • May reduce the chances of bacterial or viral infections
